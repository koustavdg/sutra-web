What's inside

Eight things, done properly.

Bring your own server

Connect Kavita, Calibre, or any OPDS-compatible server in a few taps.

Gutenberg & Standard Ebooks

Project Gutenberg's public-domain catalog and Standard Ebooks' typeset classics, ready to browse — no server required. Patrons Circle sign-in unlocks the full Standard Ebooks catalog.

EPUB & PDF

Themes, fonts, bookmarks, and in-book search, tuned for long reading sessions.

Turn pages like a book

An authentic page-curl animation, or a classic instant turn — plus a left-handed mode for one-handed reading.

Dark mode, done right

Day, Night, or follow System — book pages keep their own reading theme.

Read offline

Download books and manage storage right from the library.

Pick up where you left off

iCloud syncs progress across devices; Kavita sync is page-accurate, with series info and sorting.

Actually free

No in-app purchases. No subscription. No ads. Ever.
